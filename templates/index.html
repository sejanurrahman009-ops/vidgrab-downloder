How to Download Private Facebook & Instagram Videos

Most video downloaders only work with public videos. When you try to download a private video (one shared only with friends or specific groups), they fail with an error. This is because private videos require authentication — the downloader needs to prove it has permission to access the video.

Instagram Stories disappear after 24 hours, and there's no built-in option to save Reels to your device. VidGrab solves both problems — download any Instagram Reel, Story, or private Facebook video in full HD quality, permanently.

Method: The Page Source Trick

  • Open the private video in your browser (make sure you're logged in).
  • Right-click on the page and select 'View Page Source' (or press Ctrl+U).
  • Press Ctrl+F to search and type 'mp4' in the search box.
  • Copy the direct URL and paste it into VidGrab.
